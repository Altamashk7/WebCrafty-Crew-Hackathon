About us

About US We at Udyat, are a team of enthusiastic, highly dynamic and motivated young budding engineers, who are dedicated to make every student an expert. We are a collective group formed out of the senior students of MIET(CSE). With an enlightened mind and the will to learn, teach and share our knowledge with others, we people at Udyat give our present constantly to make a brighter future for ourselves and our fellow Means. Udyat is a team that specializes in the computer science and information technology domain. Thus, we people are the computer science society of Miet. We specializes in almost all domains of the field including python, Salesforce, Java, machine learning, android, web development, etc. At Udyat we believe that knowledge has no bounds thus, we keep organizing various events around the year for you people to test the depth of your knowledge.

Events


Codezilla

It is the biggest coding competition of miet held by our society Udyat. In this participants get some coding questions to solve. All the questions have different difficulty level. It held in many rounds. It shows the confidence of students in competitive coding And yes top performers also get exciting prizes.

Webcrafty

It is the web development. In this competition we provide a topic to the participants and they have to make their websites like indian army theme etc. It is paid competition in which top 3 performers get some exciting prizes. We focus on learning and innovation so it's not only the competition it clears the perspective of students towards web development.
